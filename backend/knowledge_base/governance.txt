GOVERNANCE & COMPLIANCE

Account Policies:

- One account per user (shared accounts not allowed)
- Password must be at least 8 characters with letters and numbers
- Accounts lock after 5 failed login attempts (unlock after 15 minutes)
- Session expires after 30 minutes of inactivity

Security:

- 2FA available for all accounts
- Suspicious activity alerts sent via email
- Regular security audits performed

Escalation Levels:

- Level 1: Support agent handles directly
- Level 2: Manager review required
- Level 3: Compliance/Legal team involved

Support Response:

"Our security policies are in place to protect your account. If you have specific questions about account access, security, or escalation procedures, please let me know and I'll help."
