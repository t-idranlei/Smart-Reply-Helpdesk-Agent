ACCOUNT SETTINGS

Customers can manage their account settings anytime.

Profile Settings:
- Change name, email, phone number
- Update profile picture (max 2MB)
- Change display language

Security Settings:
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- View recent login activity
- Change password
- Manage active sessions

Notification Preferences:
- Email notifications (on/off)

Privacy Settings:
- Manage data sharing preferences
- Request data export
- Request account deletion (30-day retention)

How to Access:
1. Log in to account
2. Click profile icon (top right)
3. Select "Settings" from dropdown
4. Navigate to desired section

Important Notes:
- Some changes require email verification
- 2FA setup requires authenticator app
- Changes take effect immediately
- Account deletion is irreversible after 30 days

Support Response:
"To update your account settings, go to Settings > Profile in your dashboard. You can change your name, email, password, and notification preferences there. Let me know if you need help with any specific changes."
